Who Are We?

Chessmate Minds Club (CMC) is a community of chess enthusiasts at CMRIT. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting your journey, our club provides a platform to learn, compete, and grow.

Our mission is to nurture critical thinking, foster friendships, and promote chess as a strategic, enjoyable pastime for students.
